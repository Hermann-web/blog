Objective

Focus on data-driven decision making (please see also the program flyer and follow us on linkedin to stay updated).

  • The aim of this day is to provide participants (research students or professionals) with a high-level training on Decision Making Driven by Data, by offering advanced courses, practical workshops and conferences on the methodologies, tools and concrete applications of this approach.
  • The spring school also aims to encourage meetings and exchanges between participants, creating an informal atmosphere that allows for networking and the emergence of opportunities for collaboration and new avenues of reflection.

Audience

  • Designed for engineers, managers, researchers and PhD students in business and industrial engineering looking for methodologies, tools and concrete applications of data-driven decision
  • Also company executives and decision-makers, who are familiar with traditional decision support tools and are wondering where to start and how to exploit the opportunities that exist hides behind the data they have or that is available to them on the net for faster and more informed decisions
