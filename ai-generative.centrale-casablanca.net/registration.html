Registration


You can register to the winter school !

However, we only have 500 places.

If you are interested, please fill that form . We will get back to you soon

Please note that your registration will be completed only once we provide you with your badge.

This registration is free of charge and, once confirmed by your badge reception, it authorizes you to participate in the conferences, the posters sessions and the round tables.

However, your transportation, meals and accommodation are entirely at your expense.

As we have received registration requests exceeding the number of potential attendees, we are currently processing those requests

To help attendees, UM6P gives to whom is interested, the option beds and meals on site. The cost of those options will be the following:

  • 200 dhs per night (single room)
  • 25 dhs per breakfast
  • 42 dhs per lunch
  • 42 dhs per dinner
Please note that the number of accommodation is limited (first come first served).

You can contact that email adress for more informations: ECC_data2022@centrale-casablanca.ma
