Poster


Poster option is only available for students and phd-students from Ecole Centrale Casablanca, EMINES and Polytechnique.

We would like students to submit title and abstract (10-15 lines) which will then be reviewed by a committee

Eventually, we will choose 20 posters in total (10 per day) all institutions combined. All doctoral students / engineering students can submit

Here's the timing

  • Submissions until January 10
  • Decision of the Committee on the 17th
  • Selected students have 10 days to complete their poster

What's included in your Poster?:

  • Description of the project
  • Results
  • Value Added
  • Bibliography with references in the project
